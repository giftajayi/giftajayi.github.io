Project Overview

This dashboard delivers a comprehensive analysis of sales performance across time, location, and marketing campaigns. It offers strategic insights into profitability trends, top-selling regions, and product-level pricing. The report is designed to assist sales managers, marketing analysts, and decision-makers in identifying high-performing periods, locations, and products to drive targeted actions.

Key Insights

  • Most Profitable Year: 2014 recorded the highest total profit, indicating strong sales momentum and effective strategies during that period.
  • Most Profitable Day: Wednesday emerged as the top day for profit generation, ideal for midweek marketing pushes.
  • Top-Performing City: San Antonio, TX was the most profitable city, suggesting strong demand or effective operations.
  • Profit by Campaign & Region: Central region campaigns, especially with IDs below 10, consistently outperformed others.
  • Sales Profit by State: Texas, California, and Florida were top contributors, as seen in a treemap visualization.
  • Product-Level Analysis: Includes metrics like Unit Cost and Unit Price for margin analysis and product profitability insights.

Business Metrics

  • Total Profit Analyzed: $8.4M
  • Top Campaign ROI: 38% in Campaign ID 8
  • Avg. Unit Profit Margin: 21.6%
  • Highest Profit Category: Electronics – avg. $187.5 profit per unit

Recommendations

  • Focus midweek marketing efforts on Wednesdays to capitalize on peak profit trends.
  • Replicate early Central region campaign strategies in underperforming areas.
  • Reallocate inventory from low-margin to high-profit states like TX, CA, and FL.
  • Upsell products in high-margin categories like Electronics and Home Appliances.

Tools & Process

Data Import & Structure

  • Sales data imported from structured Excel/CSV files.
  • Fields included: Year, Quarter, Campaign ID, Region, State, City, Product ID, Unit Cost, Unit Price, and Sales Profit.

Data Transformation (Power Query)

  • Converted dates to fiscal quarters/years, flagged nulls, formatted currency & percentages.
  • Unpivoted and filtered data to enable time-series analysis.

Data Modeling

  • Fact Table: Sales transactions by product & region
  • Dimension Tables: Products, Dates, Locations, Campaigns
  • Built star schema for slicer interactivity and optimized performance.

DAX Measures

  • Used MAXX, SUMMARIZE, TOPN for KPI generation.
  • CALCULATE + RANKX for profitability rankings by day/year.
  • IF() and SWITCH() for contextual metric highlighting.
  • RANKX() to dynamically rank profits by campaign, region, and state.
